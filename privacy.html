Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

1. Overview

JCY.Studio games (ColorFlow, Sopas de María, Juliet’s Dilemma) are designed with privacy as a default. We do not collect personal information, display advertising, or share data with third parties.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data. Game progress is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not operate servers that store user data.

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. JCY.Studio does not receive or store your payment information. Purchase receipts are verified on-device using Apple’s StoreKit framework.

4. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs. We do not track gameplay behavior, session length, or device identifiers.

5. Children

Our games do not collect data from any users, including children. They are suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above and note the change on our website.
