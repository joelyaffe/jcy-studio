A goalie game for people who love cats

Crazy Cat Soccer puts a black cat with a small white chest patch between the posts and asks a simple question: how long can you keep them out? Drag the cat anywhere on screen to intercept incoming objects. The faster you react, the more you block. The more you block, the higher your combo.

It starts like a sports game. Soccer balls, tennis balls, basketballs — manageable. Then the fish arrive. Then the watermelons. By the time the laser pointer shows up, all bets are off. Your cat will follow it. That’s not a bug.

Inspired by a real field hockey goalie named Juliet, and the very reasonable hypothesis that cats would be excellent at this if they could just focus.
