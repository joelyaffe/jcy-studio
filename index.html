An honest note about how these games are made: JCY.Studio is an experiment in a new kind of creative authorship. The code, the art, the music, and the writing in these games are generated by AI — Claude, primarily — under the creative direction and supervision of a single human. The vision, the taste, the quality bar, and the values are human. The execution is AI. We think that’s worth saying plainly, because we think you deserve to know what you’re buying.
