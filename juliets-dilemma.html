The grid has other plans

Juliet’s Dilemma takes the classic color-path puzzle and breaks its rules. Where other games give you a square grid, Juliet’s Dilemma gives you donuts, diamonds, hourglasses, L-shapes, and more — 100 hand-validated levels across 8 difficulty tiers that challenge you to think differently about space and connection.

Draw paths, lift and resume, let the synthesized music carry you through. Built on a 4-channel procedural music engine with neon visuals that pulse with every solved pair, this is a puzzle game that rewards the stubborn and surprises the experienced.
