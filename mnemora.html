Memory, reimagined

Mnemora takes the classic memory card game — flip, match, remember — and wraps it in something worth stopping for. Five illustrated card decks span celestial charts, botanical illustrations, ocean scenes, wildlife, and geometric art. Find the matches and the world comes into focus.
