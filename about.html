Made by people, made with care.

JCY.Studio is an independent game studio built on a simple belief: puzzle games should respect the people who play them. That means no ads interrupting your thinking, no data harvested in the background, and no mechanics designed to exploit your attention.

Every game we make starts with the same question: is this worth someone’s time? We care about simplicity — not as a limitation, but as a discipline. About thoughtfulness in how a puzzle feels to solve. About the quiet satisfaction of a problem that asks you to think differently.

Privacy isn’t a feature we advertise. It’s a default. Our games collect no data, require no accounts, and work entirely offline. When you buy a JCY.Studio game, you own it — completely and permanently.

We believe the best games are small, honest, and made with care. That’s what we try to build.
