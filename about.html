Made with care. Made with AI. Made honestly.

JCY.Studio is an independent game studio built on a simple belief: puzzle games should respect the people who play them. That means no ads interrupting your thinking, no data harvested in the background, and no mechanics designed to exploit your attention.

Every game we make starts with the same question: is this worth someone’s time? We care about simplicity — not as a limitation, but as a discipline. About thoughtfulness in how a puzzle feels to solve. About the quiet satisfaction of a problem that asks you to think differently.

Privacy isn’t a feature we advertise. It’s a default. Our games collect no data, require no accounts, and work entirely offline. When you buy a JCY.Studio game, you own it — completely and permanently.

How these games are actually made

JCY.Studio is also an experiment. The code, the graphics, the music, and the writing in these games are generated by AI — primarily Claude — working under the creative direction of a single human. The vision is human. The values are human. The taste, the quality bar, the decision about what ships and what doesn’t — human. The execution is AI, supervised at every step.

We think that’s worth saying plainly. The games industry — and the software industry, more broadly — is going through a fundamental shift in how things get made. We’re not hiding from that. We’re building in it, openly, to see what’s possible when a single person with clear creative intent can direct AI to build something they couldn’t have built alone.

If that changes how you feel about the games, we understand. But we’d rather you know than not.

AI-made or not, the commitments are the same: no ads, no tracking, no dark patterns, no subscriptions. Games that are genuinely designed to be played, not to be monetized against you.

We believe the best games are small, honest, and made with care. That’s what we try to build.
